Our Story

Las Cazuelas de Miguel

Welcome to Las Cazuelas De Miguel Catering services. We are a small family owned business in Burbank that offers catering services in Los Angeles with a unique twist of authentic Mexican cuisine served in traditional Cazuelas.

Simmered & Prepared in Traditional Earthenware Cazuelas

The dishes you see are served in authentic clay Cazuelas. Cazuela comes from the Arabic word meaning bowl, but today cazuela refers to other versions of the Spanish earthenware cooking vessels as well. These clay vessels, beautiful and full of history, are also utilitarian. We are passionate about providing a truly unique and delicious experience for our customers.
